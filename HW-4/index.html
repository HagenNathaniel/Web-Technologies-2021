Dungeons and Delicacies



Upon leaving the inn following her harrowing adventure against a young Troll, Silvia the tiefling rogue is exploring a small wooded area a ways away from the road to the next town.
She comes across the entrance to a cave, which looks to be an unexplored dungeon! "I should probably check this out, it looks promising!" she thinks to herself.
She thinks about what weapon to go with during her delving. A set of daggers, or her bow.
